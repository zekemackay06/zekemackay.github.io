Cubing

Speedcubing is a discipline centered about solving the Rubik's Cube (and other such puzzles) as fast as possible. If you're interested in finding out more about why I would spend my time doing this, I'd recommend checking out the documentaries Why We Cube (2018) on Youtube or The Speed Cubers (2020) on Netflix. The World Cube Association is a volunteer-run organization that sanctions speedcubing competitions across the globe.

My proudest accomplishments as a speedcuber include

  • A 17th-in-the-world Rubik's Cube time in 2022
  • Placing 28th at the 2023 World Championship in Incheon, South Korea
  • being the 8th best 'all-rounder' (skilled at all 17 of the WCA's sanctioned events) in the world for much of 2023
